**BrainDigits** is an elite, fast-paced online multiplayer number guessing game. Engineered with the **Digital Vanguard** aesthetic—a high-performance, glassmorphic UI—players compete in real-time to solve numerical puzzles. Use logical deduction, neural sync hints, and strategic escalation to outsmart your opponents or the machine intelligence.

![BrainDigits Preview](https://imglink.cc/i/ccCdYmCDE9)

## 🚀 Key Features

### 👤 Cybernetic Profiles & Progression
- **Secure Neural Auth**: Custom authentication powered by Supabase.
- **Dynamic Tier Ranks**: Climb from *Rookie* to *Legend*. The UI evolves its color palette and glow effects as you ascend.
- **Arcade Tech Tree**: Track your *Max Level*, *Total Score*, and *Mission Stars* across the Infinite Solo campaign.
- **Custom Identity**: Choice of retro arcade avatars or instant high-performance image cropping for custom profile pictures.

### 🎮 Mission Modes
- **Infinite Solo (Arcade)**: A grueling survival mode where you push your limits across infinite levels. Features persistent level-unlocking and star-rating systems.
- **Arcade Lounge (Multiplayer)**: High-speed multiplayer matches with curated arcade rules.
- **Play with Friends**: Create private encrypted tactical rooms or join via 5-character bridge codes.
- **AI Duel**: Challenge a sophisticated Bot intelligence in a localized tactical environment.

### ⚙️ Tactical Parameters (Advanced Settings)
- **Escalation Protocol**: Enable "Auto-Increase Range" to automatically expand the search area after every successful round.
- **Neural Capacity**: Dynamic guess limits tailored to the current difficulty level.
- **Pulse Timers**: Custom turn-based time limits (10s, 15s+) to force rapid-fire decision making.
- **Lives System**: A punishing HP-based system where failed guesses or timeouts drain your neural links.

### 🎨 Digital Vanguard Aesthetics
- **Kinetic UI**: Scanning animations, shimmer effects, and 3D glassmorphism.
- **Atmospheric Audio**: Independent control over synthesized SFX and immersive looping retro-synth soundtracks.
- **Global Intelligence**: Real-time leaderboards split into *PvP Masters* and *AI Slayers*.

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Visit the Arena**: Open [BrainDigits](https://braindigits.vercel.app).
2. **Sync Identity**: *Sign In* to preserve your arcade progress and global rank, or enter as a *Guest*.
3. **Select Mission**: Choose between **Infinite Solo**, **Arcade Lounge**, or a **Private Duel**.
4. **Configure Parameters**: Adjust the Range, Escalation, Timers, and Lives in the compact Mission Command modal.
5. **Deduce**: When your turn is active, submit your guess. Use the "Higher/Lower" telemetry to guide your next breach.
6. **Victory**: Guess correctly to resolve the level, earn stars, and climb the global leaderboards.

## 🛠️ Intelligence Stack

### Neural Frontend
- **React 18** — High-concurrency component architecture

- **Vite** — Optimized tactical build system
- **Tailwind CSS** — Utility-first styling for "Digital Vanguard" aesthetics
- **Lucide React** — Minimalist vector iconography
- **TanStack Query** — High-performance server-state synchronization

### Tactical Backend
- **Supabase** — Backend-as-a-Service for Auth, PostgreSQL, and Storage.
- **Real-time Sync** — Low-latency multiplayer orchestration via Websocket Channels & Presence.
